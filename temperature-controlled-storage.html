Protect Your Belongings with Our Temperature-Controlled Storage Solutions

When it comes to storing sensitive items, temperature control is essential. At Flagler Movers, we offer premium temperature-controlled storage options to safeguard your belongings from extreme heat, cold, and humidity. Whether you’re storing antiques, electronics, artwork, wine, or any other temperature-sensitive items, our state-of-the-art climate-controlled storage facilities provide the perfect environment to keep them safe, whether they are in long-distance shipping or freight storage.

What Items Benefit from Temperature-Controlled Storage?

Artwork and Antiques:

Protect from warping, cracking, or fading during long-distance moves or self-storage.

Furniture:

Prevents wood from warping and leather from cracking, ideal for moving and freight storage.

Electronics and Appliances:

Safeguard against moisture damage or overheating, perfect for freight storage and long-distance shipping.

Important Documents:

Maintains the integrity of papers and photographs, making it ideal for long-distance storage or self-storage needs.
